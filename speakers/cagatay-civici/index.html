Cagatay Civici

Cagatay Civici is a member of JavaServer Faces Expert Group, the founder and project lead of popular PrimeFaces Component Suite and PMC member of open source JSF implementation Apache MyFaces.

He’s a recognized speaker in international conferences such as JavaOne, SpringOne, JAX, Jazoon, JSFSummit and various JUG events. Cagatay is also an author and technical reviewer of couple of books regarding web application development with Java and JSF. As an experienced trainer, he has trained over 300 developers on Java EE technologies mainly JSF, Spring, EJB 3.x and JPA. He is currently working as a consultant, mentor and instructor for Prime Teknoloji in Turkey.
